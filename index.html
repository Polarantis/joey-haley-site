Joey Haley (b. 1974) is a Canadian contemporary visual artist. Blending figuration and abstraction, his work creates strange, energetic worlds that feel both familiar and uncanny. Emerging from the 1990s underground comics scene, his drawings and paintings have been exhibited internationally, including in New York, Paris, and Berlin.
